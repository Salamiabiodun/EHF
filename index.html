Educational Herald Foundation

EHF is a non-governmental organization (NGO) committed to transforming education in Nigeria. With a focus on quality, inclusivity, and holistic development, the foundation strives to create a brighter future for students and communities.

Mission and Objectives
The foundation's mission is to bridge educational gaps, promote employability, and empower students through various initiatives.

Over the years, the Educational Herald Foundation has positively impacted countless lives. Its programs have reached urban and rural areas alike, fostering a culture of lifelong learning and empowerment.

Academic Assistance

From tutoring to study materials, the foundation assists struggling students. It collaborates with schools and educators to identify areas where additional support is needed.

Infrastructure Development

The foundation actively contributes to building and improving school infrastructure. Whether constructing classrooms, libraries, or computer labs, it ensures conducive learning spaces.

Educational Camps and Bootcamps

The foundation hosts immersive camps and bootcamps where students engage in hands-on learning experiences. These events combine academics, leadership development, and recreational activities, nurturing well-rounded individuals.
